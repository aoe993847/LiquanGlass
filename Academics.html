Oracle Academy

The University joined Oracle Academy in 2005 as the first institution in Taiwan to become a member. In 2019, the College of Management COM took full advantage of its Institutional membership benefits by adopting Oracle Cloud Tier through the Oracle Academy Cloud Program. Thus, enabling teachers and students to harness their skills and get hands-on experience with Oracle Cloud Infrastructure. Since then the college has advanced and offered students with the Oracle Database 12c Administrator certificate exam, emphasizing the full set of concepts and skills that Database Administrators DBAs need for cloud computing. Recognizing Oracle Academy as the de facto curriculum for database administration and programming, the COM has created what it calls the ‘Oracle School,’ a cross-departmental concept embracing the learning resources associated with Oracle technologies. It serves not only as an umbrella for Oracle instruction but also as an intermediary for preparing teachers of the highest quality. The Oracle School is part of the Artificial Intelligence Center, AI+ Center, which integrates Oracle Academy database management and Cloud courses with Big Data, Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning with AI teaching and institutional research. In addition, the school offers courses in other Oracle applications, including Primavera Project Management, etc. The course curricula is centered on object-oriented programming (Java), database, and emerging technologies including artificial intelligence and machine learning, big data, and more. It is also well-supported by the programs such as Education Bytes, Oracle Academy Application Express, Workshops in a Box, etc. Oracle Academy Education Bytes is a modular learning resource that includes hands-on labs, projects and challenges, and videos and presentations. Students can use many of these as self-directed learning. Oracle Academy Application Express (APEX) workspace is utilized by educators and students to work in the Oracle Database Cloud, plus technical support. Notably the partnership between the University and the Oracle Academy has been recognized, awarded, and financially supported by the Taiwanese Ministry of Science and the Ministry of Education, which has blessed it with accolades for fostering information technology talent. The collaboration has been praised for its Higher Education Sprout Project, designed to encourage faculty to apply effective strategies to maximize student learning and nurture talent. Furthermore, the college keeps abreast of new Oracle technologies through online training in Oracle Academy curriculum and maintains close contact with Oracle’s offices in Taiwan. Experts regularly visit the campus to conduct seminars. Moreover, Oracle volunteers visit the Oracle School to discuss with students the types of roles available in IT, talk about their own career paths, and provide mentoring and coaching. Since the inception of the program, more than 3,000 students at the university have taken Oracle database management courses. Of these, 1,000 have acquired Oracle certification proficiency.

Center for Regional Transportation Development and Research

The University joined Oracle Academy in 2005 as the first institution in Taiwan to become a member. In 2019, the College of Management COM took full advantage of its Institutional membership benefits by adopting Oracle Cloud Tier through the Oracle Academy Cloud Program. This development center is sponsored by Ministry of Transportation and Communication. It seeks to establish and improve the public safety and transportation of local region in cooperation with the local government. In this development center, students are engaged to analyze the typical transportation problems and design an effective mechanism to overcome the challenges. In this center, students are required to actively engage with the civic body to understand the geographic terrain and develop a print of the living density map to analyze the existing track route of the public transportation system and to effectively find the optimal path. Under this center, an E-payment system is one of the prime innovative solutions designed by the group of students. Development of Virtual Reality training system for the motorcycles is also one of the innovative solutions designed to improve the public safety. The impact of this development center has been appreciated by the Ministry as there has been 20% increase in the number of passengers commuting to the new track route as compared to the previous route. Moreover, it has effectively reduced traffic accidents and improved public safety. It has also assisted numerous clients to achieve ISO 39001 Certification for the Road safety.

Financial Information Center

The Finance Information Center (FIC) is developed to foster students with in depth financial knowledge supplemented by technical competencies in financial modeling, investments, finance economic matrix, etc. through simulation of software as applied to the financial problems. This center gives students multi-dimensional learning opportunity through a kind of experiential learning. The various simulation software supported by the FIC are SPSS, Eviews, STATA, etc. To broaden the knowledge horizon of students in finance domain, this center offers access to databases such as TEJ and C-MONEY. The engagement process involves students to compute data sets using the aforementioned financial database. Acquiring to make right investments along with an effective investment strategies are the foremost highlights of this center. To stimulate innovation, the center ensures students have the ability to grasp and analyze financial data sets using the software and correspondingly accomplish the task by producing effective solution to the financial problems. The meritorious students are selected to participate and represent the college in the virtual investment competition which is based on real time simulation of the financial data. This competition is a month long and the reward in the form of impact of winning the competition is a cash prize of $ 20,000NTD.

Cross Border E- Commerce Center

This center is quintessential in developing students with the knowledge of e-commerce and smart commerce practices facilitated with the simulation of software. Majorly, freshman and senior year students actively engage in this development center as a group to participate in the major e-commerce competitions held across the region. Few of the national competitions are Alibaba, Taiwan Trade and Annual e-commerce star competition. This development center stimulates creativity and innovation as the students are tasked to present innovative solutions to the problems addressed during the competition. Few of the various tasks include designing and managing websites, content management skills, etc. Impact in the form of winning the competition gives the group an exciting opportunity to work as an apprenticeship for the organizing industry.

Interactive learning and Innovative Center

The Interactive Learning and Innovative Center (ILIC) has been established by the COM to foster the quality and efficiency of education through implementing new educational models and technologies. Student-centered pedagogy, which combines interactive learning, Team-based Learning (TBL), and Problem -based Learning (PBL), is implemented and encouraged in this center. Different from traditional lecturing methods, this new teaching space engages students to actively participate in discussions and absorb management knowledge from theory and practical issues via online and offline learning. A smart classroom is adopted in order to incorporate active learning in this center. Learners can interact with the teacher and collaborate with their peers instantly via the instant response system. The smart lecture capture system is implemented to record lectures automatically via multiple cameras so that lectures can be saved in the database. The lecture capture system is integrated with a cloud management system; therefore, students can review the video clips after each class to ensure the effectiveness of learning via the i-Learning system. In addition, the cloud learning platform maintains records of each participant, so that instructors can analyze the results and provide personalized feedback and support. The establishment of ILIC enables teachers to adopt novel teaching approaches and enhances the interaction between teachers and students. In addition, innovative practices in management education research projects are also conducted.

Faculties

The COM maintains a diverse and qualified faculty for each degree program by strategically deploying competent and proficient faculties who collectively and individually possess robust experience through initial academic preparation or initial professional experience, and who demonstrate sustained academic and professional engagement activities. As the mission statement of the COM highly regards excellence in teaching, the college hires and maintains a roster of faculty that are academically and /or professionally qualified, and engaged. As of 2020, there are more than 60 full-time faculties.

Majority of our faculties holds a Doctorate (Ph.D.) degree and is specialized in their respective field. Furthermore, many of them have gained rich experience through years of academic and research excellence. The faculties' continual pursuit of study and practice of innovative theories allow for fresh and original course content that further supply students with the tools and ideas to enter society. At present, the implementation of a policy that increases classes through advancement allows us to accept even more outstanding students and increase the number of full time professors.
